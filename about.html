Why Software Development?

By the time I was getting ready to graduate highschool, I had to very quickly decide on what I wanted to do with my life. With not much time to spare, I wracked my brain trying to figure out what seemed interesting to me. What were some things that I liked that could also be a potential career? Over the span of one month, I went over the list of my interests and hobbies that could lead to an answer, when it dawned on me. How about video games? Growing up, I used to watch my older brothers play video games, and started enjoying them myself in my teens. Had I ever thought about how they are made? No. Would it be interesting to learn? Absolutely! Within the span of two months, I finally decided my goals for the future. That is, to eventually become an indie game developer.
